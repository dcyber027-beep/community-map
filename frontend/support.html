How to Report Content

Anyone can flag content for review directly in the app — no account needed:

  • Open an incident, street note, or chat message.
  • Tap the ⚑ Report link, choose a reason, and add optional details.
  • Our moderators review reported content and can hide or remove anything that breaks the rules.

You can also email the address above if a report, street note, image, or chat message is inaccurate, abusive, privacy-invasive, or otherwise unsafe.

Community Guidelines

To keep Melbourne Community Map safe and useful, please:

  • Be accurate. Don't post false, misleading, or deliberately alarming reports.
  • Be respectful. No threats, harassment, hate speech, bullying, or graphic content.
  • Protect privacy. Don't share others' personal information or images of people without permission.
  • Keep it relevant. No spam, advertising, or illegal activity.
  • In an emergency, call 000 — this app does not replace emergency services.

Content that breaks these guidelines may be hidden or removed, and repeat abuse may be blocked. Full details are in the Terms and Community Rules.
