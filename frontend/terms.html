Purpose

Melbourne Community Map is for short-lived community awareness. It is not a replacement for police, emergency, legal, medical, or professional advice.

Community Rules

  • Do not post threats, harassment, hate speech, bullying, or graphic content.
  • Do not post private personal information, doxxing, or images of people without permission.
  • Do not make false, misleading, defamatory, or deliberately alarming reports.
  • Do not use the chat or reporting tools for spam, advertising, or illegal activity.
  • Call 000 for immediate emergencies in Australia.

Moderation

Administrators may edit or remove reports, notes, highlights, images, or chat messages to protect users and keep the app focused on community safety awareness.

Availability

The app may be unavailable during maintenance, hosting outages, backend cold starts, or network problems. Map data, geocoding, and community reports may be incomplete or delayed.

Contact

For support or moderation requests, use the contact details listed on the Support page.
